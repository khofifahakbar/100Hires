Does this resonate with you? You created a piece of B2B content you were genuinely proud of, and it ticked all the boxes. 

And then… nothing.

No one internally used it.
No real impact.

You move straight onto the next piece, only to have history repeat itself. 

All your best work, essentially, is flushed down the toilet, never to be seen again. 

This is what most B2B content teams are dealing with right now.

Not a lack of content. A lack of return on the effort that’s already gone into it. 

Because the problem isn’t always what you’re creating. It’s what happens after you hit publish.

In this week’s podcast, I sat down with Lee Densmer to unpack what content waste actually looks like inside B2B organisations and why so much good work never realises its full potential.

If you’ve ever felt like your content should be doing more than it is… this one’s worth your time.
