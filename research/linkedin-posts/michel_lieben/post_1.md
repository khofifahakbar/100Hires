lemlist just hit $50,000,000 ARR.

Here's every tool they use to run their business:

Their CEO, Charles, & their Partnerships Manager, Julia, were kind enough to share it all 👇

1️⃣ Organic Content

10% of the team regularly posts on LinkedIn.
Combined, they have over 400K followers.

This includes Erwan (40K), Yann (7K), Domitille (7K), their Founder Guillaume (46K), their CEO Charles (37K) & (plently!) others.

Their stack:
- Taplio, for LinkedIn analytics, content ideation and calendar planning.
- Figma to design compelling visuals.

They attract hundreds of thousands of people to their website monthly. Their SEO stack:

- Storyblok, their content management system (CMS).
- Semrush and Ahrefs to steer their keyword + topic research.
- Google Search Console to track organic performance.

2️⃣ Cold Outreach (Outbound)

lemlist is a leading all-in-one platform to run multi-channel outreach campaigns.

Naturally, it's their most-used product for prospecting, covering:

- Cold Calling
- LinkedIn DMs
- Email outreach
- LinkedIn voice notes
- Waterfall enrichment

They also pull data from additional platforms:

- Advanced Data sourcing: Clay.
- Intent signals: Clay, Snitcher, G2.
- AI: OpenAI, Claude, Mistral AI, Dust.
- B2B Databases: People Data Labs, Crunchbase.

3️⃣ Product Stack

They rely on a handful of platforms for production data:

- RudderStack and dbt Labs, their customer data platforms for data transformation.
- Holistics Data, their BI platform for analytics.
- Google Cloud to deploy their applications.
- BIGQUERY, their data warehouse.

Their prototyping stack:

- Replit to develop + deploy software.
- Vercel V0 for user interface + code generation.

4️⃣ Conversions & Analytics (Funnel)

Around 30,000 people sign up to their platform each month. 
(That's 1,000 signups a day!)

Their inbound marketing stack:
- Webflow to host their website.
- Default for automated inbound lead routing.
- Customer.io for email marketing sequences.

Their analytics stack:
- Mixpanel + Google Analytics (website and product metrics).
- Hightouch, their reverse ETL platform, to sync data across every department.

5️⃣ Deal Closing (Sales)

Their team added ~$12M in new ARR in the last 6 months.

Their stack:
- HubSpot (CRM)
- Claap (meeting recordings)
- lemcal (meeting scheduler)
- Hyperline (contracts, quotes, invoices)

6️⃣ Partnerships, Paid & Attribution

- PartnerStack to manage their affiliate program.
- Crossbeam for partner selling and account mapping.
- Fibbler to identify which organizations engage with their LinkedIn ads.

7️⃣ Finance

- Stripe (payment processing)
- Qonto (business account)
- Pennylane (accounting)
- ProfitWell (subscription analytics)

8️⃣ Customer Support & Team

- Slack (team comms)
- Notion (team collaboration)
- n8n (advanced automation)
- Intercom (customer support)

And that's finally it!
