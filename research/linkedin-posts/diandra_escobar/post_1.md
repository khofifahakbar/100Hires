One newsletter. 29 pieces of content. 6 platforms. Zero extra writing.

Brandon Smithwrick 🧠 went from three full-time jobs to a content queue that runs while he sleeps.

One weekly newsletter goes in. 29 platform-ready pieces come out across LinkedIn, Threads, X, Instagram, Substack, and YouTube.

But the workflow only works because Brandon spent serious time on the part everyone skips: a master prompt that defines his voice, goals per platform, and writing rules before the automation touches anything.

You can't systematize a voice you haven't defined yet.

Sara Stella Lattanzio and I broke down his full system (with the actual prompt) in this week's Workflow.
